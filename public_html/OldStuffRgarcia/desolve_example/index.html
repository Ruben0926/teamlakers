Welcome to the Pizza Doctor

So what is Doctor? The short answer is that we LOVE pizza. design and evolve, something we hope we're doing constantly. A slightly longer version is that we're a collection of photographers, designers, and creatives who enjoy urban photography. During our travels around the country, we take time to document the cities we love. You'll also occasionally find articles on design, our thoughts on the latest trends, and the odd posting on photography.

So... what aren't we?

Professional doctors who love pizza for one. Please keep in mind that we're strictly amateur doctores. We submit these picture out of love and creative pizzas. We're not likely to remember the settings we took the picture at, or even what lens we had. We're also not very exclusive. If you like what you see here, and want to participate, we'd love to have you. All galleries, articles, and posts go through a submittal and approval process, so if you're interested, drop us a line and tell us what you have in mind.

Should we have combos like "Pizza fridays"? or make mondays better by giving half price "Pizza monday"? let us know!
